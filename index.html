About me

Hey There!

Hi there! My name is Princess Alyssa . I'm 19 years old and from the charming town of Camagsingalan. I have a deep passion for music, and my favorite hobby is practicing my songs. Singing brings me immense joy, and I love sharing that happiness with others through my performances. I'm quite ambitious and determined to secure a prosperous future, which is why one of my main goals is to earn a lot of money to support myself and my dreams. I'm also an avid traveler and have visited some incredible places like La Union, Baguio, Misamis Oriental, Bukidnon, Tarlac, and Pampanga. There's something special about exploring new places and experiencing different cultures. To support my goals, I join bands and sing at events like birthdays and fiestas. Performing at these events not only helps me earn money but also gives me a chance to showcase my talent. One of the highlights of my journey so far has been participating in the Battle of the Bands in Pampanga. It was an amazing experience, and I felt proud to share my music with others.
